Chairman Message about the aim of NGO


"It is a women skill development organization which serve as woman income generation source for economically devasted, disabled , abused, widowed, divorcerd, single and outcasted woman in Pokhara Nepal since 2069 B.S. Paribartan Samaj Nepal takes the charges to provide free vocational skill training and work opportunties. After arriving at Paribartan Samaj Nepal, the women are provided with training and material cutting, sewing, weaving, dying, business management and various other skill training programs related to women employment for women empowerment."

Dr. Saroj Dhimal
